“There is so much beauty in the ordinary things that surround me. Admiring the highlights on the dishes. Looking out the window: how joyfully the light is playing in tree branches. Flowers, fruits, animals, people - aesthetics is everywhere!

I can not keep away from the inspiration the world is charging me with. That is why - painting is my life! Let my paintings bring you pleasure, I painted it with love.”

Oil Painting Artist

Ukrainian Union of Artists Member (2016 - 2019)

Active entrant in art contests & exhibitions (2009 - 2019)

Graduated Kharkiv State Academy of Design and Arts (2012 - 2018)

Artist and teacher in Kharkiv Art School (2008 - 2012)
